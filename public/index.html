    Rules

    Goal: have the strictly lowest hand total when you declare. Score 0 if right, +20 if wrong. Players are eliminated at the score limit. Last one standing wins.

    Card values

    • Ace = 1
    • 2 – 10 = face value
    • J / Q / K = 10

    Valid discards

    • Single card
    • Pair (2 of same rank)
    • Four of a kind
    • Sequence of 3 consecutive ranks (any suits). Ace low (A-2-3) or high (Q-K-A); no wrap.
    • Sequence of 5 consecutive ranks (any suits)
    • Flush of 5 (same suit)

    3 of a kind is NOT valid.

    On your turn

    1. Select cards to discard and one card to pick (from the deck or any face-up discard card), then click Move.
    2. Or click Declare if you think you have the strictly lowest total.

    Declaring

    • Strictly lowest → you score 0; each other player adds their full hand total.
    • Not lowest → you take +20; each other player gets max(0, their hand − your hand).

    Other

    • 5–6 player games use two decks.
    • Deck runs out → reshuffle discards (except current top).
    • Each round, declare unlocks only after every player has played their first turn that round.
    • Turn timer (if enabled): 60s; otherwise a random card is auto-played.
